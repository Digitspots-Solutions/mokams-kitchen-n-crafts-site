What We Offer

🎂

CUSTOM CAKES

Wedding cakes, birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, corporate cakes – we design and bake custom cakes for every occasion. Choose from chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, fruit cake, and more. Every cake is uniquely crafted to match your theme and taste preferences.

🥖

BAKERY ITEMS

Fresh daily bread, pastries, meat pies, sausage rolls, chin chin, puff-puff, doughnuts, cookies, and more. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or events. We use quality ingredients and traditional recipes that Lagosians love.

🍽️

CATERING SERVICES

Professional catering for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, conferences, and private parties. We offer Nigerian dishes, intercontinental cuisine, small chops, and refreshments. Our team handles everything from menu planning to setup and service.

CRAFTS & DECORATIONS

Handmade craft items, event decorations, souvenirs, gift hampers, and creative designs for your special occasions. We create beautiful, memorable pieces that add that personal touch to your celebrations.

Bulk Orders & Delivery

We accept bulk orders for offices, schools, and events. Reliable delivery service available across Alimosho and surrounding Lagos areas.
